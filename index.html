Coaches Corner

Meet our head coach and CEO Paul Roller

Paul grew up involved with sports and fitness throughout his teenage year and decided to pursue a career in the fitness industry. He received a Bachelor’s degree in Exercise Science and a Master’s in Exercise Physiology, and during that time, he fell in love with coaching and training for CrossFit, to which he has been dedicated to ever since. Paul has an obsessive passion for learning the latest techniques in strength and conditioning and has successfully applied them to his training programs. Coming from a heavy research-based education, he believes that anecdotal evidence is simply not good enough. That’s why Paul bases his knowledge on scientifically researched and well-tested protocols. Paul spends his time as a coach at CrossFit Outbreak, coaching classes and designing training programs for general population clients and competitive athletes alike. Paul himself is a high-level competitive CrossFit athlete, so he understands what it’s like to be the athlete and the coach, which he believes his personal experience with the challenges athletes are faced with daily gives him an advantage.
